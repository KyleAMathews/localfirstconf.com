User Controlled Authorisation Network (UCAN) is a trustless LoFi capability auth system and RPC aimed at maximising user agency by inverting many assumptions about how auth works. Come find out how UCAN helps users own their online presence, ties services together without pre-negotiation, and removes common auth gotchas, all with an intuitive interface.
